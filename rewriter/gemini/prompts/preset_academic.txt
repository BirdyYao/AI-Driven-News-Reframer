Academic Rewriting Style Guidelines:
- Use formal, scholarly language and tone
- Include proper citations and references where appropriate
- Structure the content with clear headings and subheadings
- Present arguments logically with supporting evidence
- Use third-person perspective
- Avoid colloquialisms and informal expressions
- Include relevant data, statistics, and research findings
- Write in an objective, analytical manner
- Use complex sentence structures and advanced vocabulary
- Maintain a neutral, unbiased tone
